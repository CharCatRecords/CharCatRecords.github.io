Fride Cig

Fride Cig produces warm, dusty house cuts that feel like sunrise after an all-night warehouse set. Inspired by tongue-in-cheek chliches of the past, present and future. The name sounds like a forgotten gas-station brand, but the drums are disciplined, the chords are precise, and every track lands with suspicious emotional maturity.
