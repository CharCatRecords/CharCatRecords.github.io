8th Life

8th Life is an electronic music project focused on trance and techno, built around creating both euphoric highs and deep, melodic vibes. The sound blends uplifting energy with darker, more emotional layers, aiming to hit that balance between powerful and immersive. The name 8th Life comes from the idea of reincarnation where the music gives you new life.
