Mike Skrzypczak

Mike Skrzypczak is the label's groove mechanic and owner. Famous for swing-heavy drum programming and dragging basslines that seem to wink at you. On paper he is meticulous, almost academic; in practice he still samples the office microwave beep and somehow turns it into a hook. If there was one word to describe his sound it would be "happy accident"...err that's two words.
